About the Family

Angel De La Torre was born in 1963 in a small town 50 miles east of Guadalajara called Capilla De Guadalupe in the state of Jalisco. Angel is the 3rd oldest in a family of ten. His father pulled him out of school in 3rd grade to work and support the family. He was raised in the country-side and was used to hard working environments until he was 17 years old. He made the painful decision of leaving his family to come to the United States. In 1981, two days after arriving in the U.S., he started working as a bus boy at Brentwood Country Club in Los Angeles. He then attended school in Inglewood for "English as a Second Language." Two years later, he started to work as a waiter for Brentwood Country Club. Nine years later, he met his future wife, Maria in the same town he was born in. In 1990, Angel and Maria got married and lived in Los Angeles until 1993. They then moved to Palm Springs, CA to raise their family of six, three boys and three girls. Angel has worked in the Palm Springs area for Mission Hills Country Club and Morning Side Country Club where he waited on ex-President Gerald Ford and his wife, Betty. In December 2007, he decided to open his own restaurant, La Perlita Mexican Food with his wife and kids. They all work hard to provide excellence and quality. They look forward to take care of their customers. Angel, Maria, and their kids, Carolina, Jose, Leticia, Diwan, Bianca, and Moises look forward to taking care of you personally, at La Perlita Mexican Food.
